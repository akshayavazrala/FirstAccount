# FirstAccount AI

## Overview

FirstAccount AI is a banking customer acquisition and onboarding prototype designed for hackathons and innovation challenges.

The platform helps banks identify potential customers, segment them into meaningful groups, and provide personalized banking recommendations through an intuitive digital experience.

This project focuses on creating a modern and professional banking user interface that demonstrates how AI-powered customer acquisition can improve engagement and onboarding for underserved customer segments such as gig workers, students, freelancers, and first-time banking users.

---

## Problem Statement

Traditional customer acquisition methods often rely on generic marketing campaigns and manual onboarding processes, resulting in:

* High customer acquisition costs
* Low conversion rates
* Limited personalization
* Difficulty reaching underserved customer segments

---

## Proposed Solution

FirstAccount AI provides a streamlined customer acquisition experience through:

* Customer segmentation
* Lead prioritization
* Personalized banking recommendations
* Conversational onboarding interface
* Digital-first customer engagement
